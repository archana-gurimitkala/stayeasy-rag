# StayEasy for Hosts

## How to List Your Property

### Step 1: Create Listing
- Add property details (type, location, bedrooms, bathrooms)
- Upload at least 10 high-quality photos
- Write a compelling description
- Set house rules

### Step 2: Set Your Price
- Nightly rate
- Cleaning fee (one-time)
- Extra guest fee (optional)
- Weekly/monthly discounts (optional)

### Step 3: Choose Booking Settings
- Instant Book: Guests book immediately
- Request to Book: You approve each booking
- Minimum/maximum stay requirements

### Step 4: Publish
Your listing goes live and appears in search results.

## Host Requirements
- Verified identity (government ID)
- Valid payout method (bank account or PayPal)
- Accurate listing information
- Response to inquiries within 24 hours

## Host Fees
StayEasy charges hosts a 3% service fee per booking.

Example:
- Nightly rate: $100
- 3 nights = $300
- Host fee (3%): $9
- You receive: $291

## Payout Schedule
- Payouts sent 24 hours after guest check-in
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- Available methods: Bank transfer, PayPal

## Host Responsibilities
- Keep calendar up to date
- Respond to inquiries promptly (within 24 hours)
- Ensure property matches listing description
- Provide clean, safe accommodation
- Be available for guest questions during stay

## Host Protection
- $1,000,000 property damage protection
- $1,000,000 liability insurance
- Secure payments through StayEasy
- Guest verification before booking
