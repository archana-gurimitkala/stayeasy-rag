# StayEasy Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking Questions

### How do I book a property?
Search for your destination, select dates, choose a listing, and click "Book Now"
or "Request to Book." Follow the payment steps to confirm.

### What's the difference between Instant Book and Request to Book?
- Instant Book: Reservation confirmed immediately
- Request to Book: Host must approve within 24 hours

### Can I book for someone else?
Yes, but the guest's name must be added to the reservation. You remain responsible
for the booking and any damages.

### How far in advance can I book?
Most hosts allow bookings up to 12 months in advance. Some accept bookings up to
24 months ahead.

## Payment Questions

### When am I charged?
Full payment is collected when you book (for stays under 28 nights). For longer
stays, 50% is charged at booking and 50% two weeks before check-in.

### Is my payment secure?
Yes. All payments are processed through our secure, PCI-DSS compliant platform.
Never pay hosts outside StayEasy.

### Can I pay with cash?
No. All payments must be made through the StayEasy platform using accepted
payment methods.

### When do I get my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days for credit cards, 3-5 days for PayPal.

## Host Questions

### How much does it cost to list on StayEasy?
Listing is free. We charge a 3% service fee on each booking.

### When do I get paid?
Payouts are released 24 hours after guest check-in and take 3-5 business days to process.

### What if a guest damages my property?
You're protected by our $1,000,000 property damage protection. Document the damage
with photos and file a claim within 14 days of checkout.

### Can I set my own house rules?
Yes. You can specify rules about smoking, pets, parties, quiet hours, and more.

## Safety Questions

### How are guests verified?
Guests must verify their email, phone number, and provide government ID for certain bookings.

### What if I don't feel safe?
Contact our 24/7 emergency line: 1-800-782-9111. For immediate danger, call local
emergency services (911) first.

### Are there cameras in the property?
Hosts must disclose all cameras in the listing. Cameras are prohibited in private
spaces (bedrooms, bathrooms).

## Cancellation Questions

### How do I cancel my booking?
Go to "Your Trips," select the reservation, and click "Cancel Reservation."

### What's the cancellation policy?
Each listing has its own policy (Flexible, Moderate, or Strict). Check before
booking.

### What if the host cancels?
You'll receive a full refund and rebooking assistance. The host may face penalties.

## Account Questions

### How do I contact support?
- In-app: Help Center > Contact Us
- Email: support@stayeasy.com
- Phone: 1-800-STAYEASY (1-800-782-9327)

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Note: Active bookings must be
completed or cancelled first.

### Can I have multiple accounts?
No. Each person may only have one StayEasy account.
