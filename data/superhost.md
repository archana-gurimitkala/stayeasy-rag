# StayEasy Superhost Program

## What is a Superhost?
Superhosts are experienced, highly-rated hosts who provide exceptional hospitality.
They earn a special badge on their listing and profile.

## Superhost Requirements

To become a Superhost, you must meet ALL criteria:

### 1. Experience
- Completed at least 10 bookings OR
- 3+ bookings totaling 100+ nights in the past year

### 2. Response Rate
- 90% or higher response rate
- Respond to inquiries within 24 hours

### 3. Cancellation Rate
- Less than 1% cancellation rate
- No cancellations in the past year (exceptions for emergencies)

### 4. Overall Rating
- 4.8+ star average rating

## Superhost Benefits

### Visibility
- Superhost badge on listing and profile
- Priority placement in search results
- Featured in "Superhost" collection pages

### Earnings
- Average 22% more bookings than non-Superhosts
- Guests filter for Superhosts
- Higher trust = more bookings

### Rewards
- $100 travel coupon each year you maintain status
- Early access to new features
- Priority customer support
- Exclusive Superhost webinars and tips

### Recognition
- Annual Superhost awards
- Featured host stories on our blog
- Invitation to host community events

## Superhost Assessment

### When It Happens
- Every 3 months (January 1, April 1, July 1, October 1)
- Based on performance in the past 12 months

### Losing Superhost Status
If you fall below requirements:
- Status removed at next assessment
- Badge removed from listing
- Can regain status by meeting requirements again

## Tips to Become a Superhost

1. **Respond quickly** - Reply within a few hours, not days
2. **Keep calendar updated** - Avoid cancellations due to double-booking
3. **Exceed expectations** - Small touches make big impressions
4. **Communicate proactively** - Send check-in instructions early
5. **Be honest** - Accurate listings lead to happy guests
6. **Ask for feedback** - Learn and improve continuously

## Superhost Statistics
- 15% of StayEasy hosts are Superhosts
- Superhosts earn 60% more on average
- 92% of Superhost guests leave 5-star reviews
