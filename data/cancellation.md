# StayEasy Cancellation Policies

## Guest Cancellation

Hosts choose one of three cancellation policies:

### Flexible
- Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before check-in
- 50% refund if cancelled less than 24 hours before check-in
- No refund after check-in

### Moderate
- Full refund if cancelled 5+ days before check-in
- 50% refund if cancelled 1-5 days before check-in
- No refund if cancelled less than 24 hours or after check-in

### Strict
- Full refund if cancelled 14+ days before check-in
- 50% refund if cancelled 7-14 days before check-in
- No refund if cancelled less than 7 days before check-in

## How to Cancel (Guests)
1. Go to "Your Trips"
2. Select the booking
3. Click "Cancel Reservation"
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

## Host Cancellation

Hosts should NOT cancel confirmed bookings. Penalties include:
- $50-$100 cancellation fee
- Blocked calendar for those dates
- Warning on listing ("Host cancelled X bookings")
- Potential Superhost status loss
- Account suspension for repeated cancellations

### Extenuating Circumstances (Hosts)
Penalty-free cancellation allowed for:
- Property damage making it uninhabitable
- Emergency repairs (plumbing, electrical)
- Government travel restrictions
- Natural disasters

## Refund Processing
- Credit card: 5-7 business days
- PayPal: 3-5 business days
- StayEasy credit: Instant

## Extenuating Circumstances (Guests)
Full refund regardless of policy for:
- Death of guest or immediate family member
- Serious illness or injury (documentation required)
- Government-declared emergencies
- Natural disasters affecting destination
- Military deployment
