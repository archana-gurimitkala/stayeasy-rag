# StayEasy Payments

## Guest Payments

### When You Pay
- Full payment at time of booking (stays under 28 nights)
- 50% now, 50% two weeks before check-in (stays 28+ nights)

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- StayEasy gift cards

### Payment Security
- All payments processed through secure StayEasy platform
- Never pay hosts directly outside the platform
- PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
- Funds held until 24 hours after check-in

### Payment Issues
If payment fails:
- Check card details and expiration date
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact your bank for authorization
- Try alternative payment method

## Host Payouts

### When You Get Paid
- Payout released 24 hours after guest check-in
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

### Payout Methods
- Bank transfer (ACH) - Free
- PayPal - Free
- Wise (international) - Free

### Minimum Payout
- $25 USD or equivalent

### Payout Schedule
- Daily, weekly, or monthly (you choose)
- Set your preferred day for weekly/monthly payouts

## Security Deposits

### How They Work
1. Host sets deposit amount ($100-$5000)
2. Guest's card is authorized (not charged)
3. After checkout, host has 14 days to file claim
4. If no claim, authorization released
5. If damage, StayEasy reviews and charges guest

### Disputing a Charge
Guests can dispute damage claims within 7 days. StayEasy mediates disputes.

## Tax Documents
- Hosts earning $600+ receive 1099 tax form (US)
- Available in your account by January 31
- International hosts: Check local tax requirements
