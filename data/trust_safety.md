# StayEasy Trust & Safety

## Identity Verification

### Guest Verification
- Email verification (required)
- Phone verification (required)
- Government ID (required for some bookings)
- Profile photo (recommended)

### Host Verification
- Government ID (required)
- Phone verification (required)
- Address verification
- Business license (where required)

### Property Verification
- Verified photos (StayEasy photographer)
- Address confirmation
- Random quality checks

## Reviews

### How Reviews Work
- Guests and hosts review each other after checkout
- 14-day window to submit review
- Reviews are published simultaneously
- Reviews cannot be edited after publishing

### Review Guidelines
- Be honest and accurate
- Focus on the experience
- No hate speech or discrimination
- No false claims

### Removing Reviews
Reviews are only removed if they:
- Contain hate speech or threats
- Are factually false and provable
- Violate privacy (sharing personal info)

## Safety Features

### For Guests
- 24/7 emergency support: 1-800-STAYEASY
- Share trip details with friends/family
- In-app safety center
- Neighborhood safety info

### For Hosts
- Guest screening and verification
- Secure payments (no cash handling)
- $1,000,000 liability insurance
- $1,000,000 property damage protection

### Emergency Support
Call our urgent safety line: 1-800-782-9111
- Available 24/7
- For immediate safety concerns only
- Average response time: Under 2 minutes

## Prohibited Conduct

### Guests Must Not:
- Throw parties without permission
- Exceed guest limits
- Smoke in non-smoking properties
- Damage property intentionally
- Harass or threaten hosts

### Hosts Must Not:
- Discriminate against guests
- Enter property during stay without permission
- Install hidden cameras in private spaces
- Misrepresent listing
- Cancel repeatedly

## Reporting Issues
1. Go to your reservation
2. Click "Report Issue"
3. Select issue type
4. Provide details and evidence
5. StayEasy investigates within 48 hours

## Account Suspension
Violations may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban
- Legal action (severe cases)
