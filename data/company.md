# About StayEasy

## Our Story
StayEasy was founded in 2021 by Maria Rodriguez and James Chen in San Francisco.
After struggling to find authentic local experiences while traveling, they built
StayEasy to connect travelers with unique homes and local hosts.

## Mission
"Make everyone feel at home, anywhere in the world."

## Key Stats
- Founded: 2021
- Headquarters: San Francisco, CA
- Employees: 450
- Listings: 500,000+ properties
- Countries: 85 countries
- Guests served: 5 million+
