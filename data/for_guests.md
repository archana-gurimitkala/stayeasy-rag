# StayEasy for Guests

## How to Book

### Step 1: Search
Enter your destination, dates, and number of guests. Use filters for:
- Price range
- Property type (apartment, house, villa, cabin)
- Amenities (WiFi, pool, kitchen, parking)
- Instant Book vs Request to Book

### Step 2: Review Listing
Check photos, description, reviews, and house rules. Look for:
- Superhost badge (experienced, top-rated hosts)
- Verified photos
- Response rate and time

### Step 3: Book
- Instant Book: Confirm immediately
- Request to Book: Host has 24 hours to accept

### Step 4: Payment
Pay securely through StayEasy. We hold payment until 24 hours after check-in.

### Step 5: Check-in
Host will send check-in instructions 3 days before arrival.

## Guest Requirements
- Verified email and phone number
- Valid payment method
- Profile photo (recommended)
- Government ID for some bookings

## Guest Fees
- Service fee: 10-14% of booking subtotal
- Cleaning fee: Set by host (shown before booking)
- Security deposit: Some hosts require this (refundable)

## Guest Protection
- 24/7 support if issues arise
- Rebooking assistance if listing not as described
- Refund protection for covered cancellations

## House Rules
Each listing has specific rules. Common rules include:
- No smoking
- No parties or events
- No pets (unless specified pet-friendly)
- Quiet hours (usually 10 PM - 8 AM)

Breaking house rules may result in extra charges or account suspension.
